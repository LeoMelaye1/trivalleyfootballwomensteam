Our Story

We're Just Getting Started

The Tri-Valley Women's Flag Football League is a brand-new, grassroots league created to give women in the East Bay a place to compete, connect, and have a blast on the field. Whether you played in high school, grew up watching football, or have never snapped a ball in your life — you belong here.

We are a women's-only league, period. Our mission is to build a safe, empowering, and genuinely fun environment where athletes of all backgrounds can show up and compete. The Tri-Valley has amazing women — and it's time we had a league to match.

🏈

What We Do

Seven on Seven.
Full Speed. No Pads.

🏃‍♀️
Flag Football

Non-contact 7v7 gameplay — fast, athletic, and exciting. We use regulation flags so it's pure skill and strategy.

📅
Summer League

Regular weekly games throughout June and July, with a playoff at the end. Evenings and weekends in the Tri-Valley area.

🤝
Community First

We're building more than a league — we're building a community. Expect postgame hangs, new friendships, and serious team vibes.

🌟
All Skill Levels

Beginners welcome. Veterans welcome. If you can run and you love competing, there's a spot for you on the field.
